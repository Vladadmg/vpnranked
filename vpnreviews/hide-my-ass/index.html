            MORE INFO

            HideMyAss is one of the biggest names on the market due to the great quality service they provide as well as the amount of free services that they provide.

            Pricing & Plans

            Like most VPN providers Hide My Ass has three different packages (1, 6 and 12 months) which all offer the same service but increasing price discounts. A one year subscription now costs only $4.99 per month, and even the regular pay-monthly plan is down to $9.99. If you plan on using it in the long term though then we definitely recommend the 12 month package and if you change your mind there is always the 30 day money back guarantee available for all plans.

            Features

            The company provides 860 servers with 310 locations across 190 countries – providing a possible 117,020 IP addresses – and this number is constantly growing. They also offer the three most common protocols: LLTP, L2TP and OpenVPN and easily allows you to change between them (depending on device).
            They have software available for all major operating system and because they allow 2 simultaneous connections, something that’s not very common, you do not have to worry about which of your devices you wish to use at a given time.
            On top of this they provide a vast range of free services through their website, which we will talk about later on in the post.

            Security & Privacy

            From a technical viewpoint it is great service supporting all major protocols: OpenVPN, L2TP and PTPP, using 256-bit encryption except for the latter which uses 128-bit. Their free services also show how big their dedication to internet security and anonymity is.

            The website

            If you can look past their donkey logo gimmick they definitely have an easy to use and see through site where you can see all the features and services they provide as well as blogs, posts and reviews relating to their services.

            Support

            The company has a very friendly support team that is available during most of the week (though not 24/7) and can answer any questions you may have – they even maintain their gimmicks. Due to their popularity they also have a well maintained support and community website which means that setting up or using any of their services is flawless.

            The Process

            Signing Up

            Signing up is a simple process requiring only an email address and payment. Once you have signed up they will provide you with links to download the software for your chosen device.

            Other Platforms

            The company provides support for all major operating systems and has a dedicated iOS app for your Apple products. For Android you can use the built in accessibility and they also have a Beta App for which you can trial.

            Android

            To get the Android app you have to sign-up using your Google+ account which is annoying but required for you to be able to get the download from the Play Store. Surprisingly the app is very simple and only allows you to change your location (always using the OpenVPN protocol).

            Free Services

            Firstly, there is a free online web proxy service, though very basic it comes in useful for some anonymous web searches and accessing some – otherwise blocked – webpages. Keep in mind that it is much more useful to sign up to their VPN account.

            Conclusion

            We liked

            • 591 Servers in 69 countries (and growing)
            • No Bandwidth restrictions
            • Great VPN Client
            • Lots of freebies
            • Great website

            We weren’t so sure about

            • VPN protocol set-ups (primarily L2TP)

            We hated

            • Nothing

            In overview it is clear from our tests that HideMyAss has put in plenty of effort into their company. Their Windows client is one of the best we have seen and has a lot of useful features that help improve the user experience. Their mobile clients are slightly lagging behind on this, however, they are rapidly improving this so watch the space. And with all of the freebies that they provide we can definitely say that HideMyAss is worth using (if you’re not too worried about secuirty that is).
